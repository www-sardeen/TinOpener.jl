# TinOpener.jl

![](sardines.jpeg)

TinOpener is the Swiss army knife of the [WWW-SArDEEn](https://evazehentner.wordpress.com/www-sardeen/) project at the University of Zurich.

Its main features at the moment include:

- processing of historical corpora and datasets extracted therefrom
- time series analysis
- causality and complexity measures
